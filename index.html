  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.

  7. Serve warm and enjoy!
